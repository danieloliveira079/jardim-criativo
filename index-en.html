About Jardim Criativo

The idea behind Jardim Criativo was born out of the will of a group of people to act in the social context focusing on education. That's when we decided to take into schools: agile practices, ways of working in teams and organizing activities, creative approaches for identifying and rsolving problems, among other things that are part of the daily life of companies, especially technology ones. Jardim Criativo's mission is to guide students through a journey where they'll work on themes brought by the students themselves and came out of their family or social background, or simply interest. Through the program's 12 gathering the student will identify challenges and then, in a very creative and iterative way, with collaboration, empathy development and the support of technology they'll reach possible solutions.

We believe everyone can, just like the flowers of a garden, grow and make the world around them more colorful and full of life. All it takes is a little care, the correct nutrients and attention.

Every individual has their own value and importance in our society!

Frequently asked questions

Frequently asked questions about Jardim Criativo

1 What's Jardim Criativo?

Jardim Criativo is a non-profit social project made by a group of friends with a goal in common: taking to the classrooms creative dynamics focused on identifying and solving problems, transforming them into challenges to be worked on collaboratively, helping with learning.

2 Who are the people making Jardim Criativo happen?

Our group is made up of volunteers calling themselves "Jardineiro Criativo", they're the ones who'll be on the field in the schools sowing the seeds of this rpoject. Today this groups contains software developers, agilists, a teacher and a psychologist.

3 How can I participate in the project?

The first class is already ongoing in the Escola Municipal Herondina Medeiros Zeferino, in Ingleses! Keep an eye out for our site, where we'll spread the word on which schools we'll act. Maybe yours is next?

4 How can I contribute?

At first our expenses are related to supplies like: A4 sheets, markers, flipcharts and post-its. We're always in need of more supplies the keep project going and since we don't have any kind of funding, investment or external resources, all the expenses are being paid by the Jardineiros Criativos. If you'd like to help somehow, just send an email to contato@jardimcriativo.org letting us know that you're interested in contributing with the project, and we'll get back to you soon.

5 How can I take Jardim Criativo to my school?

The Jardim Criativo is being sown, happenning at the momento only at Herondina Medeiros Zeferino school. That doesn't mean your school can't receive a seed soon, if you're interested in taking Jardim Criativo to your school, send an email to contato@jardimcriativo.org and soon we'll get in touch with you.

6 Do I have to pay to be part of the project?

No, every and any participation in the project is not related to any kind of payment or reward. Everyone involved in the organization and execution of Jardim Criativo are volunteers, and participants pay absolutely nothing.

7 What will I learn taking part in the creative journey?

As a participant of the project, I'll learn how to deal with problems and situations of my everyday life in a innovative and creative way. Beyond that, I'll learn to work collaboratively, exchanging knowledge and learning with other people in favor of a common goal.

8 What will I not find in Jardim Criativo?

Here you will not find a computer course, an english course or any other subjects. You'll also not find traditional classes. Our idea is for you to develop your skills in practice, solving a problem or situation that's part of your daily life.

9 How long does the journey take in Jardim Criativo?

The journey consists of 12 gatherings that are each two hours long. In our site you'll find the Schedule available right in the initial page. The schedule isn't fixed and might change according to the needs that come up throughout the development of the project.

10 How can I know more about the Jardim Criativo project?

For more information about the project just send an email to contato@jardimcriativo.org and we'll be immensely pleased to answer any questions that might come up.
